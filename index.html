PotentiaMod Extension Bonanza

Welcome to the PotentiaMod Extension Bonanza!

Here, you can find extensions that are either sandboxed or unsandboxed. All extensions here are reviewed for safety.

All the extensions here are from TurboWarp, PenguinMod, MistWarp, and GaiaMod.

These extensions are not compatible with Scratch. Projects that use these extensions can't be opened in Scratch or uploaded to the Scratch website.

Some extensions may not work in PotentiaMod. Extensions related to Arrays or Objects are highly likely to be incompatible with Scratch mods other than PotentiaMod. Extensions that uses either generator functions (function*) as serializers/deserializers of custom types or API sandbox runners are also incompatible outside PotentiaMod.
